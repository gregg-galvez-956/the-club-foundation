# The Club Foundation (Rio Grande Valley)

A youth-focused, peer-driven environment in the Rio Grande Valley where young people dealing with addiction and/or mental health challenges can reset, think clearly, connect, and build momentum.

This repo is the public build log + working framework. It exists to document decisions, iterate in public, and create a repeatable system that can scale beyond one person.

---

## What I’m building

**Core idea:** environment > intervention.

Not a clinic. Not a rehab. Not a therapy practice.

A space designed for **sober minds** (or minds working toward sobriety) to:
- connect without stigma
- stabilize and reset
- think, plan, and build
- develop leadership and responsibility over time

**Home-base philosophy:** people don’t need to “stay in the Valley.”  
Go if you want. Build a life. Just know home will still exist here.

- `decisions/`  
  Short decision records: context → options → decision → reason → next steps.

- `research/`  
  Notes from conversations, referral gaps, local context, insights.

- `frameworks/`  
  Governance modules, safety standards, meeting formats, operating playbooks.

- `branding/`  
  Naming experiments, messaging drafts, flyers, positioning tests.

---

## Principles

- **Quiet competence:** build first, signal later.
- **Dignity:** no victim framing. People aren’t broken, they’re under-supported.
- **Safety matters:** psychological safety and community safety are non-negotiable.
- **Clarity over hype:** do what we can deliver, then expand.

---

## 90-day goals (momentum, not perfection)

2. Recruit a small advisory/research group (youth + students + community).
3. Publish short “what we’re learning” briefs (signal competence, not marketing).
4. Translate insights into simple pilots and partnership conversations.
